Our Heritage

A story rooted in Rwanda's highland farms, nurtured by tradition, and refined through craftsmanship

In the misty highlands of Rwanda, where the morning light touches the land with golden fingers, our story begins. Generations of farmers have tended these fertile hills, cultivating tea and coffee with knowledge passed down through time.

Damarara represents more than a beverage. It embodies the spirit of Rwandan soil, the dedication of our farmers, and the heritage of a nation. Each leaf is handpicked at the peak of perfection, carrying with it the essence of our homeland.

We honor tradition while embracing modern quality standards, ensuring that every cup delivers the authentic taste of Rwanda. Our commitment extends beyond the product—we empower farmers, support communities, and preserve the craft that defines our identity.

From Farm to Cup

Every step of our journey is guided by care, quality, and respect for the craft

01

Highland Harvest

In Rwanda's misty hills, skilled farmers handpick each leaf at dawn, selecting only the finest two leaves and a bud. This careful selection ensures optimal flavor and quality in every harvest.

02

Traditional Care

Leaves are carefully collected and transported from rural estates, maintaining their integrity through time-honored methods. Each bundle represents the pride and expertise of our farming communities.

03

Modern Processing

At our Kigali facility, leaves undergo precise processing that honors traditional methods while incorporating modern quality controls. This balance preserves authentic flavor while ensuring consistency.

04

Refined Packaging

Each product is thoughtfully packaged to preserve freshness and purity. Our packaging reflects the journey—from the highland farms to your table—with elegant simplicity and sustainable materials.

Our Values

Principles that guide every decision we make

Authentic Origin

Every product is 100% Rwandan, sourced directly from our highland farms. We celebrate our roots and never compromise on authenticity.

Farmer Empowerment

We believe in fair partnerships. Our farmers receive fair compensation, training, and support to build sustainable livelihoods for their families.

Quality First

We prioritize quality over mass production. Each batch undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets our exacting standards.

Community Support

Beyond our farms, we invest in local communities through education, infrastructure, and sustainable development initiatives.

Environmental Care

Sustainable farming practices protect our land for future generations. We minimize our environmental impact at every stage.

Honest Storytelling

We communicate with transparency and confidence, sharing our journey authentically without over-commercialization.
